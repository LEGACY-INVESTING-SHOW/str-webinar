What most W-2 earners miss about short-term rental taxes

The IRS treats long-term rentals as passive, which means the losses get stuck on your return and never reach your W-2 income. A short-term rental you actively manage falls into a different category altogether, and that single distinction is what lets the deductions flow straight against your W-2 instead of sitting on the shelf waiting to be used.
