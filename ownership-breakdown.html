Why a short-term rental is taxed differently than a long-term one

Long-term rentals are passive in the eyes of the IRS — the losses get trapped and can't touch your W-2 income. A correctly structured short-term rental, where the owner materially participates, is treated as active. That one distinction is why the deductions flow straight against your W-2 tax bill instead of sitting on the shelf.
